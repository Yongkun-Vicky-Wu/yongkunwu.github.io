Yongkun (Vicky) Wu is a sophomore student majoring in arts and media (media track) at Duke Kunshan University. Grown up influenced by Korean and Chinese contemporary TV series, she develops a deep interest in visual culture, critical theory (postcolonial theory in particular), queer studies, and inter-Asian popular culture. Furthermore, she is a passionate language learner who has been learning French and Korean in the past year. Currently, she is working on a critical theory project that explores the human body’s capacity to spatialize and embody the space through tactical practices in the Korean TV series, The Liberation Notes.

I love to immerse in the process of rendering paint on canvas and paper although I have literally no painting techniques. I could not draw detailed human faces or static objects since my hands are clumsy. The rose on a red-white background is one of the few pictures that I created. The graffiti is basically a montage of magazine clips. I chose those that I found aesthetically attracting, with no intention of meaning in mind when organizing the paper.
